My Story.

Why Code?

I spent almost a decade working in game development & e-commerce so I was surrounded by all sorts of programming languages. It was hard not to be intrigued but I had no knowledge beyond HTML & CSS from my early days of tinkering Xanga. The 2020 pandemic was chaos but it gave me a window of opportunity to deep dive into coding. In Jan 2021, I officially launched my coding journey by starting with an Udemy bootcamp taught by the excellent Dr. Angela Yu. I highly recommend her course to any web dev beginner!

Why AllWomen?

Having completed 85% of the online bootcamp, I realized I have a fondness for backend web development. While it was a truly rewarding 6 month journey of being a self-taught, I believe there is much to gain from being surrounded by like-minded learners and having a mentor to review my codes. AllWomen's part time in-person course allows me to have that environment while being able to juggle my full time job at Amazon & motherhood.
